My Work

Young's Slit Diffraction

By using a Matplotlib, Numpy and Pywidget, a simulation of the intensity of light bands produced by Young's Double Slit intereference can be replicated. The parameters of the simulation can be changed to produce a different pattern.The height of the graph represents how bright the bands are.

Maxwell-Boltzmann Distribution

I created an interactive plot that describes the distribution of velocities of different gases. The parameters, like the temperature, the molarity and the weight of the gas can be changed for different results New types of gas can be added as I used a class system.

Electron Propragation

I attempted to visualise the path travelled by an electron through Earth's magnetosphere. A crude attempt of trying to model the complex nature of the aurora
